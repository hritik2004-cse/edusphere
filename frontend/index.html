what is Edusphere?

EduSphere is an innovative e-learning platform designed to enhance education through AI-powered tools, personalized learning experiences, and interactive resources. It offers features like AI Study Assistant, Resume Analyzer, Career Navigator, quizzes, eBooks, and more to support students and professionals in their learning journey.

AI Text Summarizer

EduSphere includes an AI-powered Text Summarization tool that condenses long content into clear, concise summaries while preserving key details. It helps students quickly understand research papers, articles, and notes. With a copy button for easy sharing and text-to-speech (TTS) for auditory learners, EduSphere makes studying more efficient and accessible.
